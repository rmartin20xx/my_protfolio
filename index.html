Mini-Project 2

A farm-to-home website connects consumers directly with local farmers, providing a convenient platform for purchasing fresh, locally sourced products. By eliminating the middlemen, the website ensures that customers have access to high-quality produce, dairy, meats, and other farm-fresh goods while supporting local agriculture and reducing the carbon footprint associated with long-distance transportation. With features like online ordering, home delivery, and transparent information about the farmers and their practices, the farm-to-home website aims to promote a sustainable and healthy lifestyle for consumers.
